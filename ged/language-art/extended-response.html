GED Language Arts Practice

The extended response is a writing task of GED® test. Reasoning Through Language Arts (RLA) section includes two reading sections and one writing section. The extended response carries about 20% of the total score for the test.

Students are encouraged to practice with the resources as often as necessary to achieve your highest possible scores in your GED exam.
