About IGCSE

There are two IGCSE Exam Boards which are Cambridge (Cambridge International Examinations) and Edexcel (Pearson Edexcel) exam boards. International General Certificate of Secondary Education" is what IGCSE stands for. It is comparable to the GCSE exam, which UK students take in Years 10 and 11 to get ready for A-levels and more advanced pre-university coursework. The IGCSE is the ‘international’ version of this academic qualification as it’s available in many countries around the world.
